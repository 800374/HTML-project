Google's History

Google was created on September 4, in 1998. It was made by Larry Page, and Sergey Brin they were
PhD students at Stanford in Clifornia. Both of them own about 14% of is public sharesand control
over half (56%) of the stockholdervoting power. In their dorm rooms they built a search engine
that used links to determine the importance of the pages. At first they called it the Backrub.
Later Google caught the eye of a couple of investors and they (Larry & Sergey) got a check for
$100,000, and with this they upgraded from their dorm rooms to a garage in Menlo Park, California,
owned by Susan Wojcicki.

Fun facts about Google

Google is a play on word "googol." Google's first doodle was Burning Man stick figure. A caterer
for the Gratful Dead was Google's first chef. Swedish chef is a language preference in Google search.
Gmail was launched on April Fool's Day. Lastly Google negotiated its acquisition of YouTube's at
Denny's over mozzarella sticks.
